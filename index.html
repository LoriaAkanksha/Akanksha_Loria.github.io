Project Overview

YourGPT is an AI‑powered platform designed to provide a no‑code solution for building chatbots. It supports over 100 languages and is deployable across websites, apps, and social media. The project features advanced query pipelines including recursive retrievers, rerankers, and sub‑query decomposition to deliver precise, structured outputs.
